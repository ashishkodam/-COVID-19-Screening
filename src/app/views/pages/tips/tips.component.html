1.Clean your hands often, either with soap and water for 20 seconds or a hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol. 2. Avoid close contact with people who are sick. 3. Stay at home as much as possible. 4. Cover your mouth and nose with a cloth face cover when around others. 5. Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash. 6. Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces daily.
